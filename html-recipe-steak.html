Directions

    1.) Preheat stove with cast iron on medium to high heat covering with olive. oil
    2.) Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
    3.) On cuttin board salt & pepper steak on each side to your liking.
    4.) Once cast iron is hot, sear steak on each side for 2 minutes until brown.
    5.) Place seared steak on cooking tray, and place in oven for 10 minutes.
    6.)Remove steak from oven, and let sit in cooking tray for 5 minutes before serving.
